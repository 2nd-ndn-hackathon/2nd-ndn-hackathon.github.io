These are the accepted proposals for the 2nd NDN Hackathon. The retreat attendees will pick a proposals to work on at the begenning of the Hackathon. After voting, proposals with only one participant would be removed from accepted projects and the participant will have to pick another project.

3. [Mini-NDN Real-time Interaction GUI by Vince Lehman](https://www.dropbox.com/s/uo5zy875sd6jiqx/minindn-gui-proposal.pptx?dl=0)

4. [Micro forwarder Firefox add-on by Jeff Thompson](https://www.dropbox.com/s/ywjujmfcj3sxm2w/Micro%20forwarder%20Firefox%20add-on.pptx?dl=0)

5. [End­-to-­End performance testing of ndn­sci application by Chengyu Fan](https://www.dropbox.com/s/5xptnk1y8p0uquq/ndn-sci.pptx?dl=0)
